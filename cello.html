Cello Lessons

Warm, encouraging cello tuition for all ages and abilities in Stockport.

What we cover

  • Posture, bow hold and left-hand technique
  • Tone production and sound development
  • Scales, arpeggios and technical exercises
  • Classical and contemporary repertoire
  • Sight-reading and music theory
  • Chamber music and ensemble skills
  • Exam preparation (ABRSM, Trinity)

A welcoming approach

Lessons are warm, patient and tailored to you. Whether your child is just starting out, or you are an adult who has always wanted to play, you will be met with encouragement and a love of music.

Instrument hire

Not sure whether to commit to buying a cello? We can point you in the right direction for hiring a suitable instrument locally while you get started.
