Trumpet, Cornet & Flugelhorn Lessons

Brass tuition for beginners through to advanced players in Stockport.

What we cover

  • Embouchure development and breathing technique
  • Tone production and sound quality
  • Scales, arpeggios and technical exercises
  • Sight-reading and music theory
  • Classical repertoire and brass band music
  • Jazz and commercial styles
  • Exam preparation (ABRSM, Trinity)

Which instrument?

Trumpet, cornet and flugelhorn are closely related — switching between them is straightforward once you have a solid foundation. Whether you play in a brass band, orchestra, or jazz group, lessons are tailored to your goals and the music you love.

All levels welcome

Whether you are picking up a brass instrument for the first time or returning after a break, we work at your pace. Lessons are available for children and adults.
