Drum Lessons

One-to-one drum kit lessons for all ages and levels in Stockport.

What we cover

  • Posture, grip and stick technique
  • Reading drum notation and chart reading
  • Grooves across rock, pop, funk and jazz styles
  • Independence and coordination exercises
  • Fills, dynamics and musicality
  • Playing along to music and with a band
  • Exam preparation (Rockschool, Trinity)

Who are lessons for?

Complete beginners are very welcome — no kit needed to get started. Lessons are available for children (from around age 7) and adults. If you already play and want to progress or iron out bad habits, we can help with that too.

Lesson format

Lessons are 30 or 60 minutes, held in Stockport. Both options are available — shorter lessons suit younger beginners, while longer sessions give more time to explore.
